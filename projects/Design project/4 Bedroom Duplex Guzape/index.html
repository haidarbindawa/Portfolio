Project Overview

The Proposed 4-Bedroom Detached Duplex at Plot 1015, Cadastral Zone A09, Guzape, Abuja is a private residential development requiring a complete structural design package. The building is conceived as a reinforced-concrete framed structure with slabs, beams, columns and foundations designed to safely resist gravity and lateral actions in accordance with applicable codes.

Scope of Work

  • Full structural modelling of the duplex using appropriate structural software.
  • Determination and application of all design loads (dead, live, wind, roof, staircase loads, etc.)
  • Establishment of load combinations in accordance with relevant design codes.
  • Initial member sizing for beams, columns, slabs and stair elements.
  • Structural analysis and interpretation of analysis results.
  • Re-design and optimisation of structural members where required.
  • Foundation system design (pad, strip or combined footings based on load conditions).
  • Roof structural design (timber or steel truss analysis and detailing).
  • Preparation of reinforcement detailing for all structural elements
  • Bar bending schedules for contractor use.
  • Preparation of construction-ready structural drawings.
  • Compilation of structural calculation reports for submission and approval.
  • Coordination with architectural layouts to ensure buildability and alignment.

My Role & Key Contributions

  • Developed the structural model for the duplex and established design load cases and combinations.
  • Performed structural analysis and designed beams, columns, slabs and stair elements to satisfy strength and serviceability criteria.
  • Designed the foundation system based on assumed/available geotechnical inputs and structural load demands.
  • Designed the roof framing to achieve a stable, constructible and architecturally compatible configuration.
  • Prepared structural drawings, reinforcement details and bar bending schedules for contractor execution.
  • Coordinated structural layouts with architectural requirements to maintain functionality and constructability.

Outcome

The project delivered a complete structural design package for the 4-bedroom detached duplex, including calculations, structural drawings and reinforcement details ready for construction. The final design strikes a balance between safety, durability and material efficiency, while remaining coordinated with the architectural intent and suitable for residential development standards in Abuja.
