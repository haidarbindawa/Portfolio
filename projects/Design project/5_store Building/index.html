Residential Block of Flats – Mabushi, Abuja

Back to Projects

Project Overview

Full structural design of a residential block of flats in Mabushi, Abuja, covering 3D modelling, load determination, member sizing, analysis, re-design optimisation, foundation design, roof truss design, structural detailing and preparation of complete calculation reports.

Scope of Work

  • 3D structural modelling of the entire building
  • Load determination and application (dead, live, wind, and roof loads)
  • Initial member sizing of beams, columns, and slabs
  • Structural analysis and interpretation of results
  • Optimisation and re-design of members where required
  • Design of shallow foundations and ground beams
  • Roof truss structural design
  • Preparation of structural detailed drawings
  • Full structural calculation and design report
  • General structural coordination with architectural team

My Role & Key Contributions

  • Lead structural design engineer
  • Performed full structural modelling and analysis
  • Prepared all reinforcement detailing and BBS
  • Developed structural drawings for approval and construction
  • Produced the structural calculation report
  • Coordinated design interfaces and clarified structural requirements

Outcome

The final design delivered a safe, efficient, and fully compliant RC structure ready for construction. All drawings, models, and reports were issued in line with modern structural engineering practice, ensuring constructability and ease of interpretation by contractors on site.
