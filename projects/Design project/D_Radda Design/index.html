Proposed Residential Villa – Kaduna

Back to Projects

Project Overview

Structural design of a ground-plus-upper-floor residential villa for a private client in Kaduna. The design delivers safe, constructible reinforced-concrete solutions and detailed reinforcement documentation for contractor execution.

Scope of Work

  • Structural analysis and member sizing for beams, slabs, columns and foundations.
  • Foundation design and detailing based on site constraints.
  • 3D structural modelling using PROTA for analysis and load checks.
  • Preparation of reinforcement detailing and Bar Bending Schedules (BBS).
  • Production of structural drawings and submission documentation for approvals.

My Role & Key Contributions

  • Performed full structural analysis and member sizing for the villa design.
  • Developed the 3D model in PROTA and used it to verify load paths and member capacities.
  • Prepared reinforcement detailing and Bar Bending Schedules for construction use.
  • Produced structural drawings and coordinated submissions for client/approval.

Outcome

The design package delivered a coordinated set of structural drawings, BBS and analysis reports ready for tendering and construction, supporting efficient and compliant build execution.
