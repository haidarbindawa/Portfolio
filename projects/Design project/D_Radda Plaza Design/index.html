Project Overview

The project involves the structural design of a commercial plaza comprising a ground floor and two additional floors. The building is intended to support retail and office activities, ensuring safety, stability, and efficient load distribution while remaining practical for construction and future adaptability.

Scope of Work

  • Load assessment and structural analysis
  • Sizing of beams, slabs, columns, and stair elements
  • Foundation system design based on soil characteristics
  • Reinforcement detailing and Bar Bending Schedules (BBS)
  • 3D Structural Model using Prota Structures
  • Preparation of construction and coordination drawings
  • Preparation of full structural design report

My Role & Key Contributions

  • Developed the structural analysis model and verified load assumptions.
  • Performed sizing and optimization of beams, slabs, columns, and foundations.
  • Prepared reinforcement detailing and Bar Bending Schedules (BBS).
  • Generated full construction drawings and coordination layouts.
  • Worked closely with architects and builders to ensure structural practicality and constructability.

Outcome

The final structural design achieved a safe, cost-efficient, and constructible solution that met both functional and architectural requirements. Clear detailing and documentation ensured smooth site execution and contractor understanding.
