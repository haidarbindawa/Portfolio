Project Overview

The FJSC Exam Centre project involved the construction of a dedicated examination facility for the Federal Judicial Service Commission (FJSC), developed to provide a secure, functional, and durable environment for examinations and assessment activities. The building was delivered from foundation level through superstructure and roofing, with emphasis on structural integrity, quality workmanship, and compliance with approved design standards.

The construction scope included excavation and reinforced-concrete foundation works, followed by erection of the reinforced concrete superstructure, masonry blockwork, and construction of reinforced concrete walls and associated structural elements. The project also involved the fabrication and installation of steel roof trusses and framing, completing the structural envelope of the facility.

Execution of the works required careful coordination of formwork, reinforcement fixing, concrete placement, and structural inspections to ensure compliance with approved drawings, specifications, and applicable building codes. Close collaboration between the contractor, consultant, and client representatives was maintained throughout the project to resolve technical issues promptly and ensure consistent quality delivery.

The completed facility meets the functional and structural requirements of the Federal Judicial Service Commission, providing a reliable and well-coordinated examination centre suitable for long-term institutional use.

Scope of Work

  • Excavation, reinforcement placement, and concrete foundation works.
  • Construction of the reinforced concrete superstructure and masonry blockwork.
  • Installation of reinforced concrete walls and associated structural elements.
  • Fabrication and installation of steel roof trusses and framing.
  • Coordination of formwork, rebar fixing, concrete pouring, and structural inspections.

My Role & Key Contributions

  • Served as the Resident Engineer for the Consultant.
  • Supervised construction activities to ensure compliance with approved drawings and standards.
  • Verified reinforcement, formwork, and concrete placement works during implementation.
  • Coordinated with contractor and client representatives to resolve technical issues.
  • Ensured quality and structural integrity across all phases of work.

Outcome

The facility was successfully delivered to functional and structural standards, providing a suitable environment for examinations and assessments under the Federal Judicial Service Commission.
