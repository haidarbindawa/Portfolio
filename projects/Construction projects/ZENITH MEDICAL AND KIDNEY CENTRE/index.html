Project Overview

This project involved the construction of a modern hospital facility for the Zenith Medical and Kidney Centre, located at Plot 1070, Cadastral Zone B18, Sector Centre District, Abuja. The facility was developed to support a comprehensive range of medical services, with a dedicated focus on renal healthcare.

The building was designed to deliver high-quality treatment, diagnostic, and patient-care environments, with strong emphasis on functionality, hygiene, accessibility, and patient comfort. The project required close coordination of structural, architectural, and building services works to meet the stringent performance and quality expectations associated with healthcare facilities.

Construction activities were executed in line with approved drawings, specifications, and applicable building standards, ensuring the delivery of a structurally sound and well-coordinated facility suitable for medical operations and subsequent fit-out and commissioning.

My Role & Key Contributions

  • Served as Construction Engineer, supervising and managing the site workforce.
  • Ensured works were executed in accordance with approved drawings and specifications.
  • Coordinated construction sequences between civil, MEP and finishing trades to maintain progress and quality.
  • Carried out inspections, quality checks, and verified reinforcement and formwork prior to concreting.
  • Maintained progress reporting and communicated with client and consultants on technical matters.

Outcome

The project delivered a structurally robust and fully coordinated hospital facility ready for fit-out and commissioning, meeting the specified functional requirements for the Zenith Medical & Kidney Centre.
